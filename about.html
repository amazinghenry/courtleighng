OMODUNKE ADELAKUN
Managing Director

Mrs Omodunke Adelakun is a graduate of Accounting from the Ogun state University. She is an Associate of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria and an Alumnus of the Lagos Business School Senior Management Programme. She worked in the Banking industry for 29 years in various commercial banks including Chartered Bank, Stanbic IBTC and WEMA Bank where she retired as the zonal manager in charge of the Lagos Island Business Area. She has extensive experience in credit , operations, marketing as well as being a chartered accountant.
